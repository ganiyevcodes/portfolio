Birinchi o'rinda Full-Stack dasturchi bolish, hamma veb dasturlash tillarini mukammal o'rganish. Undan so'ng freelancer bo'lib, birinchi maoshimni olish. Yana IELTS`dan overall 7.5 yoki 7.5+ olish.
